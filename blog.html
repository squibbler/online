Welcome

Squibbler first started as a blog, hosted on Wordpress. But since, it has been dismantled and brought back to life as a website, hosted on Github. I searched in vain to find a hosting company that I liked, to no avail. Github was recommended by a friend, so I tried it. It has a bit of a learning curve at first, but then becomes easier to use. It allows you to record your progress, and even collaborate with others to help, if you so choose to..

Well, we are happy you decided to drop by. In the coming months we are creating our layout and content for our site, and look forward to sharing them with you.

Thanks.
